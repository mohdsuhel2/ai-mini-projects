Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NOOBius (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use noobius.in and related pages (the “Site”). The Site provides free browser-based tools to generate receipts, bills, invoices, and slips.

1. Summary

NOOBius runs as a static website. Document data you enter is processed in your browser to show a live preview and create downloads. We do not operate a user account system and we do not ask you to sign in.

2. Information stored on your device

To improve your experience, the Site may save the following locally in your browser:

  • Form field values and generator preferences (for example theme, panel width, and last-used settings)
  • Bulk preview session data while you work on a batch export
  • Uploaded images you choose for signatures, stamps, or photos (kept locally unless you clear browser data)

This information stays on your device unless you clear site data, cookies, or local storage in your browser. We do not receive your receipt or bill content on our servers when you use the standard generator workflow.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

We use browser storage (such as localStorage and sessionStorage) for site functionality. Third-party services used by the Site may set their own cookies or use similar technologies, including:

  • Google AdSense — to show advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Google Fonts — to load web fonts
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs) — to deliver scripts and stylesheets efficiently

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect ads or some site features.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on the Site. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to serve ads based on your visits to this Site and other websites.

Our publisher ID is ca-pub-2293170892331368. You can learn how Google uses data from partner sites at Google’s partner sites policy and manage ad personalization at Google Ad Settings.

Downloads on NOOBius may show a short sponsored screen before the file is unlocked. Ads shown there use the same AdSense units configured for the Site.

5. Log and hosting data

Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, requested page, and timestamp) for security, uptime, and troubleshooting. We use this data only to operate and protect the Site.

6. Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies separately.

7. Children’s privacy

The Site is intended for general business and personal document use. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your choices

  • Clear browser storage for noobius.in to remove saved form data
  • Use private/incognito mode if you do not want settings persisted
  • Adjust cookie and ad personalization settings in your browser or Google account

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions about NOOBius, contact us at admin@noobius.in.
