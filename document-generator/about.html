About NOOBius

NOOBius is a browser-based document studio with live preview, bulk generation, and export to PNG or PDF — built for teams that need consistent, professional documents fast.

What it does

Five specialized generators with enterprise-ready layouts, theming, and export options.

How it works

No install, no backend — open the generator, fill the form, download your document.

STEP 01

Choose document type

Select fuel receipt, rent receipt, driver slip, ecommerce invoice, or postpaid bill from the top navigation.

STEP 02

Edit with live preview

Every field updates the preview instantly. Single mode shows live preview; bulk mode opens a separate preview page.

STEP 03

Export and share

Download PNG or PDF, print directly, or generate batches and download as ZIP.
